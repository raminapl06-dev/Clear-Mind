Can you help me understand the Pythagorean theorem?
Of course! Imagine a right triangle like a ramp. The two shorter sides (legs) are your everyday distances — like walking 3 blocks east and 4 blocks north. The longest side (hypotenuse) is the shortcut across. a² + b² = c² tells you exactly how long that shortcut is. So 3²+4² = 9+16 = 25, meaning c = 5. Want to try one?
